AmeriPlus Products

AmeriPlus GAP

AmeriPlus Guaranteed Asset Protection provides financial protection against a vehicle's actual value versus the outstanding balance when a vehicle is declared a total loss.

AmeriPlus VSC

AmeriPlus Vehicle Service Contract offers additional levels of protection, including some of the expenses not typically covered in the manufacturer's standard warranty, including towing and rental car options.

AmeriPlus Pre-owned VSC

AmeriPlus Pre-owned Vehicle Service Contract can help extend the life of a pre-owned vehicle with five simple coverage plans designed to protect against unexpected, costly repairs after the manufacturer's standard warranty expires.

AmeriPlus TWP

AmeriPlus Tire & Wheel Protection repairs or replaces damaged wheels or flat tires and offers 24-hour protection against unexpected road hazards with no customer deductibles.

For consumer questions, contact the program administrator at 1-800-900-6227.
