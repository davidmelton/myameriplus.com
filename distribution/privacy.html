When you use our website we will store cookies on your computer in order to facilitate your use of our website. A cookie is a small data text file, which a website stores on your computer's hard drive (if your Web browser permits) that can later be retrieved to identify you to us. The cookies make your use of the website easier, make the website run more smoothly and help us to maintain a secure website. You are always free to decline our cookies if your browser permits, but some parts of our website may not work properly in that case. Also, we may obtain your internet protocol address, computer’s operating system, and browser type; the website address of a referral website; and the path you take on our web pages.

We secure personal information consistent with current industry standards. Personal information is protected in several ways. We maintain physical, electronic and procedural safeguards. We restrict access to personal information to those employees who need to know in order to provide services. The Program Administrator’s Confidentiality Policy, which applies to all employees, restricts the use of all personal information and requires the utmost discretion.

On our website, we have installed usernames, passwords and other safety features to ensure that personal information remains secure. We recommend that you do not divulge your password to anyone.
